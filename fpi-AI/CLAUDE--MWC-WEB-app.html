Systemic Racism in Europe

Systemic racism is a pervasive issue in Europe, perpetuating racial disparities and inequalities.

Mechanisms of Systemic Racism

  • Institutional Racism
  • Racial Profiling
  • Discrimination in the Labor Market
  • Education
  • Media Representation

Institutional Racism

Institutional racism refers to the ways in which institutions perpetuate racial disparities and inequalities.

Racial Profiling

Racial profiling refers to the practice of targeting individuals based on their race or ethnicity.

Discrimination in the Labor Market

Discrimination in the labor market refers to the ways in which people of color face barriers in accessing employment opportunities.

Education

Education refers to the ways in which institutions perpetuate racial disparities and inequalities through their policies and practices.

Media Representation

Media representation refers to the ways in which the media perpetuate racial stereotypes and biases.

Reparations

Reparations for Africa and African-descendant populations in Europe and the Americas could be around $50 trillion to $150 trillion USD.
